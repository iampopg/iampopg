## 👤 About Me

Cybersecurity analyst with deep expertise in **digital forensics**, **malware analysis**, and **security research**. My work revolves around understanding how threats evolve, how attackers think, and how to protect individuals and organizations — especially within the African digital space.

I also build tools and scripts designed for **marketing**, **education**, and **research**, ensuring they are safe, ethical, and practical.

> ⚠️ All public scripts are intended strictly for **ethical**, **educational**, and **research** purposes.
